User manual – GAiN-Study GO

This is a FYP project for creating interactive study games that can help students - no matter they have special learning needs or not- to learn more effectively.

This guide explains how to upload content, generate quiz questions, and play the running game.

1) Prepare and upload

  • Supported files: pdf, docx, pptx.
  • Choose number of questions, then click Generate quiz.
  • The app uploads your file to process questions; files are deleted automatically after processing. Uploads may take up to a couple of minutes; if you hit a 502, refresh and try again.

2) Pick questions

  • Review the generated list and select the questions you want.
  • When ready, click Start game.

3) How to play

  • Desktop: Use A (left) and D (right) keys.
  • Mobile / Touch: Move your finger left and right to control the car.
  • Answer each question by steering to the correct option lane.

4) Finish and survey

  • After the run ends, you’ll see your score.
  • Tap the Open survey button to provide feedback—please complete it!

Build Your Study GO

1) Create a quick two‑option quiz.
2) Choose the questions you want to play.
3) Start running and steer with touch or A/D.
